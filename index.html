Quick Start Guide

Show ▼

Skip the music theory — start playing immediately. Here's how to get started in 5 simple steps.

Step 1
Power & Volume
Slide the red power button on top. Adjust the volume slider for headphones or speakers.
Step 2
Play Your First Chord
Press any of the 7 chord buttons (1-7). Each represents a scale degree — all chords naturally work together.
Step 3
Explore Sounds
Press , then use the joystick left/right to browse different waveforms and synthesis engines.
Step 4
Add Effects
From the sounds menu, move the joystick up to enter effects. Navigate to reverb or delay and toggle them on.
Step 5
Transpose Keys
Press , then joystick LEFT/RIGHT to transpose the instrument to different keys instantly.

A pocket-sized, all-in-one music production instrument that gives anyone immediate access to thousands of chords — no music theory required.

How It Works
  • 7 Chord buttons represent the chords that naturally work together in any key.
  • Patent-Pending Chord-Modifying Joystick changes chord character in real time (major, minor, 7th, suspended, etc.).
  • Because there are no wrong notes, anyone can make music immediately.
The interface feels more like a game controller than a synthesizer — natural, playful, and instantly accessible.

Power + Capability

Despite its size, HiChord delivers the power of a full studio in one handheld instrument.

Core Engine
  • 12-voice stereo engine built on Daisy Seed platform (400 MHz ARM Cortex-M7).
  • Four sound engines: Analog synthesis · FM synthesis · Sample playback · Hybrid mode.
Production Suite
  • Drums · 16-step sequencer · 2-track looper · Arpeggiator.
  • Full FX: reverb, delay, chorus, flanger, filter, ADSR.
  • Built-in microphone for live sampling and sound capture.
Connectivity
  • USB-C audio + MIDI for instant DAW integration.
  • Massive battery life for true portability.
  • Continuous firmware updates — every batch adds new sounds, modes, and refinements.
